**The [data](https://github.com/panosgjuras/Perceived_safety_choices/tree/vosUpdates/data) folder contains:**

- [scenarioAthens/baseNetwork_v1](https://github.com/panosgjuras/Perceived_safety_choices/tree/vosUpdates/data/scenarioAthens/baseNetwork_v1): includes the base network of city center of Athens (1st, 2nd and 3rd municipal department): links and nodes,

- [models_v0.1](https://github.com/panosgjuras/Perceived_safety_choices/tree/vosUpdates/data/models_v0.1): includes the initial models computed based on 129 respondents from Athens, Greece. They do not contain random effects,

- [models_v1.1](https://github.com/panosgjuras/Perceived_safety_choices/tree/vosUpdates/data/models_v1.1): includes the updated models computed based on 129 respondents from Athens, Greece. They contain random effects,

- [models_v1.2](https://github.com/panosgjuras/Perceived_safety_choices/tree/vosUpdates/data/models_v1.2/psafe): includes the updated models computed based on 50 respondents from Munich, Germany. They contain random effects,

- [models_v2](https://github.com/panosgjuras/Perceived_safety_choices/tree/vosUpdates/data/models_v2/psafe): includes the updated models computed based on 129 + 50 respondents from both cities. They do not contain random effects,

The raw dataset is open and can be found here:
> Tzouras, P. G. (2025). Perceived safety and first/last mile mode choices. A dataset from image-based double stated preferences experiments (2.0) [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17779261

Please cite the dataset, if you use it in your analyses.
